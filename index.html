FAQ'S

Frequently Asked Questions

What type of photography do you specialize in?

I specialize in portrait, landscape, event, and commercial photography, capturing moments that tell unique stories.

How can I book a photography session with you?

You can book a session through the contact form on this site, or reach me directly by email or phone. I'll get back to you within 24 hours to discuss details and availability.

What equipment do you use for your photography?

I work with professional full-frame cameras, a range of prime and zoom lenses, and studio lighting when the shoot calls for it. The gear is always matched to the type of session.

Can I request a specific location for a photo session?

Absolutely. Whether it's a favorite spot in the city or a remote landscape, I'm happy to travel. Location ideas are part of our planning conversation.

What is your editing process like?

Every photo goes through a careful selection and editing pass — color grading, exposure correction, and subtle retouching — while keeping the images natural and true to the moment.

Are digital files included in your photography packages?

Yes. Every package includes high-resolution digital files delivered through a private online gallery, ready for download and printing.

Do you offer prints of your photographs?

Yes, prints are available for purchase. Explore the 'Prints' section for more details on sizes and pricing.

How long does it take to receive the edited photos after a session?

Standard turnaround is 2–3 weeks depending on the session type. Sneak peeks are usually shared within a few days after the shoot.
